So?

Coming from 14 years of experience across festivals and cultural centres of different scales, aesthetics, and organisational structures from emerging independent initiatives to large international institutions I collaborate with festivals, cultural centres, artists, and international networks on international collaboration, curatorial strategy, artistic exchange, partner finding, and programme development.

If you are developing a project, festival, artistic programme, international partnership, or simply looking for a conversation around an idea, feel free to get in touch.

Ways of Working

International Touring & Partnerships

I support artists and organisations in developing touring strategies, identifying potential partners, and building cross-border collaborations. This can include strategic positioning, mobility planning, partner research, and advice on approaching festivals, venues, and networks.

Curatorial & Programme Development

I work with festivals and cultural organisations on artistic programmes, curatorial frameworks, and interdisciplinary or international initiatives. This can involve developing a new programme, reviewing an existing direction, contextualising artistic work, or translating an initial idea into a coherent project.

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Access

I advise festivals, cultural institutions, and professional networks seeking to make their programmes, partnerships, and working practices more inclusive and representative. This can include reviewing an existing approach, developing a DEIA initiative, facilitating internal conversations, and turning institutional intentions into realistic actions.

Facilitation, Networks & Professional Exchange

I design and facilitate conversations, workshops, professional encounters, and collaborative structures across the performing-arts sector. My approach focuses on creating conditions for meaningful exchange, shared learning, and sustained relationships between artists, institutions, and independent practitioners.
