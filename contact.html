So?

Coming from 14 years of experience across festivals and cultural centres of different scales, aesthetics, and organisational structures from emerging independent initiatives to large international institutions I collaborate with festivals, cultural centres, artists, and international networks on international collaboration, curatorial strategy, artistic exchange, partner finding, and programme development.

If you are developing a project, festival, artistic programme, international partnership, or simply looking for a conversation around an idea, feel free to get in touch.

Supporting artists, festivals, and cultural institutions in developing cross-border projects, touring strategies, mobility frameworks, and international partnerships.

Curatorial & Strategic Advisory

Advising festivals and organisations on programming, internationalisation, contextual framing, and artistic positioning.

Programme Design for Cultural Centres

Supporting cultural centres and festivals in designing interdisciplinary programmes, international collaborations, and audience-facing formats that foster meaningful artistic exchange.

Facilitation & Public Discourse

Moderating conversations, workshops, and professional encounters around performing arts, mobility, programming, and cultural exchange.

Networks & Ecosystem Building

Designing and supporting collaborative structures, peer networks, and transnational initiatives across the performing arts sector.
