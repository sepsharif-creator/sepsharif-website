Networks & Initiatives

PADA (Producers, Agents and Distributors Alliance)

A peer network of 180 performing arts professionals across more than 30 countries.

This alliance exists to advance horizontal collaboration and knowledge-sharing. It serves as a vital infrastructure for independent producers and distributors to exchange resources, advocate for better touring conditions, and reshape the dynamics of international collaboration outside traditional institutional hierarchies.

IETM

International network for contemporary performing arts.

Member of the Advisory Committee since 2022. I served as Co-chair from 2022 to 2025, and 2026 is my final year on the committee, after contributing to the network's plenary meetings programming and inclusive strategies.

I am also actively involved in the coordinators group of the IETM Producers Group, a space for producers, agents, and managers to exchange knowledge, strengthen peer infrastructures, and reflect on producing across generations, disciplines, and borders.
