Sepehr Sharifzadeh

Sepehr Sharifzadeh builds contexts for artistic exchange: networks, platforms, and infrastructures that help collaboration move across geographies.

His work sits between curation, production, discourse, and facilitation, creating conditions where artists, institutions, and independent practitioners can meet on more thoughtful terms.

Rather than focusing on isolated projects, his practice develops frameworks for circulation, shared knowledge, and sustained international cooperation.
